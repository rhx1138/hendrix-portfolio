About

My goal is to create beautiful, usable, professional websites using industry best practices. Design is crucial in creating software people enjoy using. For this reason, my designs are simple, but effective.


I am currently living in Indianapolis, Indiana and have been involved within the research and consulting field for more than five years. I recently switched my career to Front-end Development due to an internal project at my company that I had been involved with focusing on UI.


My background in research methodology while working closely with clients towards a solution as well as my background in Graphic Design, has been highly beneficial in making such a transition into this great field.
